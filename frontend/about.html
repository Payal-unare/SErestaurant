About Us

Our Story

Since 1990, India Restaurant has been Kolkata’s landmark for authentic vegetarian cuisine. Our legacy blends traditional recipes with modern presentation, ensuring every guest enjoys flavors that feel like home yet excite the senses.

From Paneer Butter Masala to Dal Tadka, from Veg Biryani to Stuffed Paratha, every dish is prepared with love, fresh ingredients, and a touch of tradition.

We believe in serving not just food, but happiness and memories.

Our Mission

To bring the richness of Indian vegetarian cuisine to every plate, keeping traditions alive while embracing innovation in taste and style.
